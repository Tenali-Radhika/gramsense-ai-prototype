# GramSense AI - User Guide for Farmers

A simple guide to help farmers use GramSense AI for better market decisions.

---

## What is GramSense AI?

GramSense AI helps farmers make better decisions about:
- When to sell their crops
- Which market to go to
- What prices to expect
- What crops to plant next season

   - Chennai
   - Hyderabad

Wait a few seconds while the system gathers information.

## Understanding the Results

### 1. Current Price Information

You will see:
- **Today's Price**: Current market price for your crop
- **Price Change**: Whether price went up ↗️ or down ↘️
- **Percentage Change**: How much the price changed

**Example:**
```
Current Price: ₹2,500 per quintal
Change: +5.2% ↗️ (Price is going up!)
```

### 2. Price Trend Chart

A graph showing how prices changed over time.
- **Green line going up** = Prices increasing
- **Red line going down** = Prices decreasing
- **Flat line** = Prices stable

### 3. Price Forecast

Predictions for future prices:
- **7-day forecast**: Prices for next week
- **30-day forecast**: Prices for next month
- **Confidence level**: How sure the system is (Higher is better)

**Example:**
```
Next Week Prediction: ₹2,600 per quintal
Confidence: 85% (High confidence)
```

### 4. Selling Recommendation

The system tells you what to do:

**SELL NOW** 🟢
- Prices are good right now
- Prices might go down soon
- **Action**: Sell your crop today or this week

**WAIT** 🟡
- Prices might improve soon
- Wait a few days or weeks
- **Action**: Hold your crop for better prices

**CHANGE MARKET** 🔵
- Better prices available in nearby markets
- **Action**: Consider selling in a different mandi

**MONITOR** ⚪
- Situation is unclear
- Keep watching prices
- **Action**: Check again in a few days

### 5. Best Markets

List of markets with good prices:
- Market names
- Expected prices
- Distance from your location

### 6. Weather Impact

How weather affects prices:
- **Good Rain** = Helps crops, may lower prices
- **Too Much Rain** = Damages crops, may raise prices
- **Hot Weather** = Stresses crops, affects prices
- **Cold Weather** = Slows growth, affects supply

## Using the AI Assistant

### What is the AI Assistant?

A chat feature where you can ask questions in simple language.

### How to Use It

1. Find the "AI Query Assistant" section
2. Type your question in the text box
3. Click "Ask" or press Enter

### Example Questions

**About Prices:**
- "What is the price of wheat today?"
- "Will rice prices go up?"
- "Is this a good price for my onions?"

**About Selling:**
- "Should I sell my wheat now?"
- "When is the best time to sell potatoes?"
- "Which market has the best price?"

**About Farming:**
- "What should I plant next season?"
- "Is it a good time to sow rice?"
- "What crops grow well in my area?"

### Understanding AI Responses

The AI will give you:
- **Direct answer** to your question
- **Reasoning** why it suggests something
- **Confidence level** how sure it is
- **Additional suggestions** other questions you can ask

## Tips for Best Results

### ✅ Do This:
- Select the correct crop and region
- Check prices regularly (daily or weekly)
- Read the full recommendation, not just the action
- Consider weather information
- Ask the AI assistant if confused

### ❌ Avoid This:
- Don't make decisions based on one day's data
- Don't ignore weather warnings
- Don't sell in panic if prices drop slightly
- Don't wait too long if recommendation says "SELL NOW"

## Understanding Confidence Levels

**High Confidence (80-100%)**
- System is very sure about the prediction
- Safe to follow the recommendation

**Medium Confidence (60-80%)**
- System is fairly sure
- Consider other factors too

**Low Confidence (Below 60%)**
- System is uncertain
- Be careful, check multiple sources

## Common Questions (FAQ)

### Q: How accurate are the price predictions?
**A:** The system uses historical data and trends. Accuracy is typically 70-85%, but actual prices may vary due to unexpected events.

### Q: Should I always follow the recommendations?
**A:** Recommendations are guidance, not guarantees. Consider your own situation, storage capacity, and urgent needs.

### Q: What if my crop is not listed?
**A:** Currently, we support 10 major crops. More crops will be added in future updates.

### Q: Can I use this on my mobile phone?
**A:** Yes! The website works on all devices - phones, tablets, and computers.

### Q: Is this service free?
**A:** Yes, GramSense AI is completely free for farmers.

### Q: Do I need internet to use this?
**A:** Yes, you need internet connection to access the website and get latest prices.

### Q: How often are prices updated?
**A:** Prices are updated daily based on market data.

### Q: What if the website is slow?
**A:** Try refreshing the page or check your internet connection. If problem persists, try again after some time.
